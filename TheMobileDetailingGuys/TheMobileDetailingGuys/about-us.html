About Us at The Mobile Detailing Guys

Hi there!

At The Mobile Detailing Guys, we are dedicated to providing top-tier mobile detailing services in Ottawa and Gatineau. We understand the importance of maintaining the appearance and value of your vehicle, and that's why we are here to offer you the finest automotive care right at your doorstep.

We're The Mobile Detail Guys! Mobile Detail Guys has been a two-man show for three years now and we are proud to have it that way. We started this company to help car owners have affordable, quality vehicle details without having to leave their home. No one should have to settle with a messy car!

We have both always had a passion for working with cars and started out years ago picking up different techniques to make our own cars look the best they could.

We both work full-time in construction but won't let that stop us from detailing as many cars as possible. Since we both already work full time our availability can vary but we do our best to fit everything in. Last Spring/Summer (March-June 2022) we did over 100 details of various vehicles in between full-time work and school. We only get more efficient each year and plan to surpass that this Spring/Summer.
